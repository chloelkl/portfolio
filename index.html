Sustainify

Our team aimed to promote sustainability through a web application that leverages influence and incentives. I was responsible for the challenge management aspect of the web app. To achieve this, I developed the front-end using React and MUI and managed the back-end with Express and Node.js.

Drawing from my experience with previous projects, I also guided team members who were less familiar with GitHub. I took the initiative to start building the client side of the app, ensuring that the development process was smooth and efficient. As a result, the project successfully created an engaging platform that encourages sustainable practices, and my leadership helped the team become more proficient with GitHub, leading to better collaboration and development outcomes.
