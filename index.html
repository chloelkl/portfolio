2020

Kidnaped Roblox Escape Room

I proposed this RobloxStudio project during my Secondary 2 holidays to my friend, as game development sparked an interest in both of us. Our goal was to design and build an escape room experience that would not only captivate players, but also allow us to utilise Roblox Studio and scripting in Lua to experiment with coding and development as complete amateurs.

I took the lead in designing the layout of the escape room. We collaborated closely throughout the development process, overcoming numerous bugs and technical challenges by supporting each other and troubleshooting together. Our efforts led to our game accumulating over 170,000 visits to date. This project not only provided a sense of accomplishment, but also significantly fueled our interest in programming and game development.
